MTV REACHED OUT TO HAPPY COG to redesign the recently launched online music award celebration, the O Music Awards. Happy Cog needed to deliver a four stage digital experience (including a promotional blog, online voting, a live online event broadcast, and an evergreen show archive) from strategy through design to back-end CMS implementation. And it all needed to built in a matter of a few months. Using a time and money saving responsive design strategy, Happy Cog’s adaptive framework delivered a compelling experience specific to mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes from a common code base.
